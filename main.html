Marble Game Rules

  1. You have 3 minutes to complete the game.
  2. Both players start with 10 marbles.
  3. One player hides a number of marbles, the other guesses if the total is even or odd.
  4. Correct guess: You win the hidden marbles. Wrong guess: You lose those marbles.
  5. Players take turns hiding and guessing.
  6. Collect 20 marbles to win.
  7. If time runs out before anyone wins, both players are eliminated.
